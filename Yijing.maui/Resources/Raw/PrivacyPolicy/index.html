Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Yijing respects your privacy. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

Data Collection and Use

The app provides optional AI chat features. When you use AI chat, the text you enter and the AI's responses are sent to a third-party AI service solely to generate replies. The app does not retain this data after the conversation or transmit it elsewhere.

Local Storage

Apart from AI chat, the app stores files only on your device under Documents\\Yijing. These files remain on your device and are not uploaded or shared.

Data Sharing

Yijing does not collect personal information, track users, or share data with other parties.

Your Choices

You may stop using the AI chat feature at any time. To remove data saved in Documents\\Yijing, delete the folder.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact the developer through the support information provided in the app's Microsoft Store listing or via the project's repository.
